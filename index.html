üîê 2. The Consent Core

The User's Key

The citizen is the sole owner and arbiter of their Locker. All data sharing is governed by explicit, granular, and auditable consent.

  • Total Ownership: User decides what data to share (e.g., "mobile money history" only)
  • Granular Permission: User decides with whom to share (e.g., "Bank A," but not "Bank B")
  • Time-Bound Access: User decides for how long data is shared (e.g., "one-time loan application")
